About

David Bennett was born in sunny South Miami, FL. He studied visual art from a young age and attended the New World School of the Arts in Downtown Miami. He recieved his BA with a focus in art from the New College of Florida, and has worked for a local gallery, Etra Fine Art, for four years. He also covers music and culture for the Miami New Times, and does freelance illustration and design.
